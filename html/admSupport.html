Admission Support

MAT Services BD ensures a logical and well-supported journey from university or language school selection to visa approval. We guide you through every stage of the application process, ensuring that you are fully prepared and confident, whether you are applying to a Japanese university or a language school. With our expert assistance, your academic journey to Japan is in capable hands.

Language Training

MAT Services BD offers structured Japanese language training to meet proficiency requirements for admission to Japanese language schools, ensuring you're fully prepared.

Identifying Suitable Universities

MAT Services BD helps you select the right Japanese university by providing detailed information on academic reputation, programs, requirements, and deadlines, ensuring you make an informed decision.

Identifying Departments and Professors

We guide you in selecting the right departments and professors, ensuring your research interests align with theirs, which increases your chances of acceptance.

Guidance for Research Proposals

We assist you in crafting a strong research proposal that is aligned with the department's focus, increasing the likelihood of acceptance into your chosen program.

Preparing COE Documents

We assist you in preparing all necessary documents for the Certificate of Eligibility (COE) application, ensuring your visa process runs smoothly without delays.

Document Translation and Review

MAT Services BD provides expert translation and review services, ensuring your documents are accurate and culturally appropriate for both language schools and immigration authorities.
